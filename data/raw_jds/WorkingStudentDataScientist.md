Ready to shape tomorrow with data? Join us in Nürnberg as a Working Student Data Scientist (f/m/d) and turn your curiosity into real-world impact while exploring the latest in machine learning with a passionate team!


You'll make an impact by

You support our Data Science team by benchmarking machine learning models for our internal customers and contribute to the continuous improvement of our model repository
Building on this, you create prototypes, verify their performance, and work closely with the team to prepare their deployment into production environments
Additionally, you run experiments with the latest models, researching and applying state-of-the-art methods to address specific application challenges
Throughout your work, you communicate your findings clearly and visualize results to ensure transparency and knowledge sharing within the team

This is how you'll win us over

Education: You are currently enrolled in a university program in computer science, data science, artificial intelligence, mathematics or a related field
Experience and Skills:
You have a solid understanding of core software development practices, including clean code, object-oriented programming, code reviews, git, and APIs
You have experience working with high-quality data and applying machine learning algorithms to generate valuable insights
You are skilled in comparing models and presenting your findings using appropriate visualization tools
Ways of Working: You communicate your work clearly, show initiative with your own ideas, and have a strong growth mindset
Languages: You have excellent English skills; German and other languages are a plus
