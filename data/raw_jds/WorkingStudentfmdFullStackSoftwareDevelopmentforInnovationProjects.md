Do you want to help create the future of healthcare? Our name, Siemens Healthineers, was selected to honor our people who dedicate their energy and passion to this cause. It reflects their pioneering spirit combined with our long history of engineering in the ever-evolving healthcare industry.

We offer you a flexible and dynamic environment with opportunities to go beyond your comfort zone in order to grow personally and professionally. Sound interesting?

Then come and join our global team as Working Student (f/m/d) to support our Full-Stack Software Development for Innovation Projects.


Choose the best place for your work – Within the scope of this position, it is possible, in consultation with your manager, to work mobile (within Germany) up to an average volume of 60% of the respective working hours.



Your tasks and responsibilities:


You support us in developing innovative app prototypes with full‑stack skills in React Native as well as Python or Node.js
You assist us in an agile development environment and actively contribute to implementing new features
You work with Git, Docker, and Azure DevOps to implement and document our development processes
You support us in close collaboration with our stakeholders to translate their requirements into work packages
To find out more about the specific business, have a look at https://www.siemens-healthineers.com/products-services


Your qualifications and experience:


You are studying computer science, media informatics, business informatics, or a comparable technical degree program with a programming focus, and you are available for at least one year
You have practical experience in front‑end and back‑end programming languages (React Native / Expo or Node.js), ideally also Python
You have a strong passion for programming
You are familiar with agile development methods and tools such as Git, Docker, and Azure DevOps, or you are willing to learn them

Your attributes and skills:


You communicate confidently in English; German skills are desirable
You possess strong analytical thinking skills, a structured working style, and a high willingness to learn
You enjoy working in a team, are curious, and are motivated to try out and actively contribute new technologies and methods
You are willing to commute between our locations in Forchheim and Erlangen
Does that sound like you? If so, we look forward to receiving your application!
