Are you passionate about shaping the future of AI? Join us as a Working Student Leveraging Generative AI along the Software Development Lifecycle (f/m/d) in Garching and work side by side with our experts to develop innovative AI solutions, drive research, and make a real impact!


You'll make an impact by

You support the team in designing and developing generative AI solutions that enhance the software development lifecycle, leveraging your ongoing studies in Data Science, Computer Science, or a related AI/ML field
As you collaborate with senior AI developers, you assist in data collection, preprocessing, and analysis to ensure robust model training and validation
Building on this, you implement and optimize machine learning algorithms and models, applying your proficiency in Python and familiarity with frameworks like TensorFlow or PyTorch
Subsequently, you participate in code reviews, testing, and debugging to maintain the quality and reliability of AI-powered applications throughout the SDLC
Finally, you document code, processes, and results, facilitating knowledge sharing and contributing innovative ideas by staying updated on recent advancements in generative AI

This is how you'll win us over

Education: You are currently enrolled in a Master’s program in Data Science, Computer Science, Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, or a related field
Experience and Skills:
You are proficient in Python; knowledge of Java or C++ is a plus
You have hands-on experience with large language models, prompt engineering, vector databases, or fine-tuning
You are familiar with popular machine learning frameworks such as TensorFlow, PyTorch, and libraries like scikit-learn
You have research experience and strong coding skills
Ways of Working: You demonstrate strong analytical and problem-solving skills, attention to detail, and excellent teamwork and communication abilities
Languages: You possess very good written and oral English skills (C1); German skills are an advantage
