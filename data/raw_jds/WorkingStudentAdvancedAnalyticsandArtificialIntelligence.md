Ready to shape the future of data analytics and AI? Join us as a Working Student Advanced Analytics and Artificial Intelligence (f/m/d) at Munich and help drive real innovation by working on groundbreaking projects with cutting-edge technologies.


You'll make an impact by

You support the conceptual development and execution of advanced analytics and artificial intelligence projects, contributing innovative ideas to our team
In close collaboration with team members, you extract, validate, and transform data using cloud-native tools such as Snowflake and Azure, ensuring data quality and accessibility
Building on your technical skills, you help develop and maintain data pipelines in cloud environments and assist in applying machine learning algorithms with Python and relevant frameworks
Additionally, you contribute to the creation of interactive dashboards and reports, translating complex analytics into actionable insights for stakeholders
Throughout your work, you actively participate in workshops and prepare training materials on emerging AI/ML technologies, helping to share knowledge and foster learning within the organization

This is how you'll win us over

Education: You are currently successfully studying business informatics, statistics, mathematics, physics, data science, engineering, or a similar field and have completed at least four semesters of your bachelor’s degree or are pursuing a master’s/Ph.D.
Experience and Skills:
You have practical experience in analytics, data science, software development, or related areas through internships, academic projects, or research
You demonstrate proficiency in Python (pandas, numpy, scikit-learn), SQL, and have hands-on experience with cloud data platforms (Snowflake, Azure, AWS)
You are familiar with machine learning algorithms, model evaluation and deployment, version control (Git), and collaborative development workflows
Ways of Working: You are eager to learn, think analytically, and communicate complex technical concepts to non-technical stakeholders
Languages: You have excellent English skills (minimum C1 level); German is an advantage
